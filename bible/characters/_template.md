# [Character Name]

Copy this file for each major character. Filename: lowercase-hyphenated
(e.g., `jane-doe.md`).

## Role

Protagonist / antagonist / supporting. One line on their function in the story.

## Want vs Need

- **Wants:** what they consciously pursue.
- **Needs:** what they actually require to grow (often the opposite of want).

## Arc

Where they start, where they end, what changes them. Two or three sentences.

## Physical

Distinguishing details only. Eye/hair colour, build, scars, mannerisms, the
way they carry themselves. Skip generic.

## Voice

How they speak. Vocabulary, rhythm, tics, what they never say. A sample line
of dialogue helps Claude calibrate.

## Backstory

Only what affects on-page behaviour. Don't write a biography.

## Secrets

What they hide. From whom. What happens if it surfaces.

## What they know

Information they have that other characters don't, and vice versa. Critical
for keeping dialogue honest.

## Relationships

Who they care about, hate, fear, owe. One line each.
